‚ö° Quick Reference - Key Thresholds

üî¥ Red Flag Triggers

  • Current Ratio: < 1.0 (critical), < 1.2 (warning)
  • Debt/Equity: > 2.0 with negative OCF
  • Interest Coverage: < 2.0x
  • Gross Margin: < 0% or decline > 5pp
  • Days Sales Outstanding: > 90 days
  • Share Dilution: > 5% annually

üü¢ Green Flag Triggers

  • FCF Margin: > 15%
  • Growth (3yr CAGR): > 10% (all metrics)
  • ROE: > 30%
  • ROIC: > 15%
  • CapEx/Revenue: < 5%
  • Net Cash Position: Cash > Debt

üí° Remember

These thresholds are guidelines. Context matters:

  • Industry norms vary significantly (e.g., retail vs. software)
  • Company size and maturity affect appropriate levels
  • Trends often matter more than absolute values
  • Multiple flags together tell the complete story
